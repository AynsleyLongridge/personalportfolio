004 : Typography Exercise

16/12/2016 : Typography Exercise from IXD302 Narrative and Storytelling module.
×

"In order to explore how web typrography works I would like you to to find a piece of typography. Ths could be from a magazine or a famous quote. Using Google Fonts or Typekit recreate it aka Master-Apprentice from HTML and CSS."

For this exercise I recreated a Helvetica type poster I found online. After finding a similar font from Google Fonts I then had to play about with margins and z-index positions in css to layer and style a replica of the poster.
