Everyday Magic

IShared is a one off donation station that will be located in airports around the globe. You can instantly donate to support education world wide with one tap. Through contactless payment anyone can contribute without having to register any details. The magic not only comes from the accomplishment of that ‘I did something good’ feeling but also with iShared we will show you exactly how you have helped by printing a personal illustrated receipt.

The brief for this years IXD Student competition was to asking to focus on small interactions that you can improve in a massive way. Signing up to donating to various charities has all become a long process that not everyone can afford to commit to. We wanted to create something that allows one off payments with a simple tap without having to sign up or share any personal details.
