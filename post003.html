003 : India

03/12/2016 : Reflecting on my trip to Bangalore last summer.
×

I got a taster of studying in an international environment when I attended a three week course at a university in Bangalore, India last year. This was an experience that is almost too difficult for words, being outside your comfort zone seems scary, but the things that you learn exploring a new culture and meeting new types of people is invaluable and has an influence on my decisions and ideas still today.
