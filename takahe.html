National Geographic: Takahe Bird

Focusing on narrative storytelling we were given the brief to produce a single page webside based on content given to us about an extinct New Zealand bird.
×
×
×

After reading the content I wanted create some characters and illustrations that could draw a story from the text. I started my idea process when pen and paper, then once I had a rough direction I then went on to create a digital moodboard to give me further inspiration.

Once I had marked up my content and my prototypes and illustrations were designed in Sketch I then went on to building the website using HTML and CSS. Below you can take a look at the outcome!
